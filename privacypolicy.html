Privacy Policy

At Outsoon, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your information.

We are proud to declare that Outsoon does not collect, store, or share any personal data or third-party data in any form.

1. No Data Collection

Outsoon is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not:

  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

  • Access your device’s camera, contacts, or any other sensitive data.

  • Share or transmit any information to third-party services, companies, or advertisers.

2. No Third-Party Integrations

Outsoon does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that could collect or track your data.

3. No Cookies or Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, tracking technologies, or any similar tools to monitor your activity or behavior while using Outsoon.

5. Data Security

Since Outsoon does not collect or store any data, there are no security concerns related to personal information. You can use the app with peace of mind knowing your privacy is fully protected.

6. Your Rights

As Outsoon does not collect any personal data, there are no rights required to request access to, modify, or delete personal information. You can use Outsoon without needing to worry about data privacy issues.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your privacy while using Outsoon, please feel free to contact us at: admin@unicorndonkeys.com
